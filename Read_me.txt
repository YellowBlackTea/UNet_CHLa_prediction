Numpy dataset of variable

PSC stands for Phytoplankton Size Classes (Micro, Nano, Pico)

Dyn contains the dynamics (the physical predictors) : 
mlost : mixed layer depth, (uo,vo): currents, thetao : Sea Surface Temperature, 
so : Sea Surface Salinity, zos : Sea Surface Height, (u10,v10) : winds, cdir : solar flux, wmb : bathymetry

Chl contains the satellite chlorophylle-a 

nan values indicates continents and missing data
